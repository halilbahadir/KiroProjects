# Product Overview

This is a Salesforce Activities Reporting System that generates comprehensive activity reports for AWS Solutions Architects and account managers. The system analyzes Salesforce CRM data to produce detailed reports on customer engagements, workshops, architecture reviews, and strategic activities.

## Purpose

- Generate structured activity reports from Salesforce data
- Track customer engagement metrics and performance indicators
- Document technical workshops, architecture reviews, and executive meetings
- Provide insights into account management and strategic initiatives
- Support performance reviews and activity analysis for AWS field teams

## Report Structure

Reports follow a standardized format including:
- Executive summary with employee details and role information
- Activity overview with metrics and geographic focus
- Categorized activities (workshops, architecture reviews, management meetings)
- Customer portfolio analysis with key accounts and opportunities
- Performance metrics and CSAT scores
- Strategic impact areas and recommendations
- Detailed activity timelines organized by quarter

## Target Users

AWS Solutions Architects, Account Managers, and leadership teams in the Field-AGS organization, particularly focused on ISV (Independent Software Vendor) accounts in EMEA regions.
